Lock Screen Reminders

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Lock Screen Reminders is built with privacy as a core principle. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Access

The app requests access to your Reminders through Apple's EventKit framework. This access is used solely to display your reminders in the Lock Screen widget. You can revoke this access at any time in Settings > Privacy & Security > Reminders.

Data Collection

Lock Screen Reminders does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered
  • No crash reports are sent
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No third-party SDKs or services are included

Data Storage

Your reminder data is read directly from the system Reminders database on your device. The app does not copy, cache, or store your reminders separately. All processing happens locally on your device.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with anyone. The app does not communicate with any external servers. There is no network activity of any kind.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub.
