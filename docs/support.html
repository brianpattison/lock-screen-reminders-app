How to Use the Widget

  1. Open the Lock Screen Reminders app and grant access to your reminders.
  2. Select which reminders list you want displayed on your Lock Screen.
  3. Long press your Lock Screen, tap Customize, and select your Lock Screen.
  4. Tap the widget area below the clock and add the Reminders widget.
